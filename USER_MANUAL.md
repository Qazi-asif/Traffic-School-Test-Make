## 1. Introduction {#introduction}

Welcome to the Traffic School CRM system! This comprehensive platform provides state-approved online traffic school and defensive driving courses. This manual will guide you through every step of your journey, from registration to receiving your completion certificate.

### What You'll Learn

- How to create your account
- How to select and purchase a course
- How to navigate the course player
- How to complete quizzes and the final exam
- How to download your certificate

### System Requirements

- **Browsers**: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+
- **Internet**: Stable broadband connection (minimum 5 Mbps)
- **Devices**: Desktop, laptop, tablet (10" or larger recommended)
- **JavaScript**: Must be enabled
- **Cookies**: Must be enabled

## 3. Registration Process {#registration-process}

The registration process consists of 4 steps. You must complete all steps in order.

### Step 1: Basic Information

**Required Fields:**
- **First Name**: Letters, spaces, hyphens, and apostrophes only
- **Last Name**: Letters, spaces, hyphens, and apostrophes only
- **Email Address**: Valid email format
- **Confirm Email**: Must match your email exactly
- **Password**: Minimum 8 characters with:
  - At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
  - At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
  - At least one number (0-9)
  - At least one special character (!@#$%*&())
- **Confirm Password**: Must match your password exactly

**Tips:**
- Use a valid email you check regularly (you'll receive important notifications)
- Choose a strong, memorable password
- Write down your password in a secure location

**Common Errors:**
- "Email already registered" - This email is already in use. Try logging in or use a different email.
- "Passwords must match" - Ensure both password fields are identical
- "Password too weak" - Add uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters

### Step 2: Personal & Court Information

**Personal Information (Required):**
- **Mailing Address**: Your full street address
- **City**: Letters, spaces, hyphens, and apostrophes only
- **State**: Select from dropdown
- **ZIP Code**: 5 digits or 5+4 format (e.g., 12345 or 12345-6789)
- **Phone Number**: Split into 3 parts (XXX-XXX-XXXX)
- **Gender**: Male or Female
- **Date of Birth**: Month, Day, Year (must be between 1950 and current year)

**Driver's License Information (Required):**
- **Driver's License Number**: Your state-issued license number
- **License State**: State that issued your license
- **License Class**: Your license class (e.g., Class D, Class C)

**Insurance Discount Option:**
- Check "Insurance Discount Only" if you're taking the course for insurance purposes only
- If checked, court information fields become optional

**Court Information (Required unless Insurance Discount Only):**
- **Court Selected**: Name of the court handling your citation
- **Citation Number**: Your traffic ticket number
- **Court Due Date**: Month, Day, Year (current year or within next 2 years)

**Tips:**
- Have your driver's license and citation handy
- Double-check all information for accuracy
- Court information must match your citation exactly


### Step 3: Security Questions

You must answer 10 security questions. These are used for account verification and password recovery.

**Questions Include:**
1. **Q1**: License expiration year (4 digits, e.g., 2025)
2. **Q2**: Your weight in pounds (numbers only)
3. **Q3**: Number of cars you own (numbers only)
4. **Q4**: Last 4 digits of SSN or other ID (4 digits)
5. **Q5**: Your current age (numbers only)
6. **Q6**: Age when you got your license (numbers only)
7. **Q7**: ZIP code (5 digits)
8. **Q8**: Birth year (4 digits)
9. **Q9**: Hair color (letters only)
10. **Q10**: City of birth (letters only)

**Tips:**
- Answer honestly - you'll need these for account recovery
- Write down your answers in a secure location
- Use numbers only for numeric questions
- Use letters only for text questions

### Step 4: Terms and Agreement

**Required Actions:**
- **Agreement Name**: Type your full legal name
- **Terms Agreement**: Check the box to agree to Terms & Conditions

**What You're Agreeing To:**
- Course completion requirements
- Payment and refund policies
- Certificate delivery terms
- Account usage policies
- Privacy policy

**Tips:**
- Read the terms carefully before agreeing
- Your typed name serves as your electronic signature
- You must agree to proceed with registration

### Completing Registration

After Step 4:
1. Click **"Complete Registration"**
2. You'll receive a welcome email at your registered address
3. Your account is created with "Student" role
4. You'll be redirected to:
   - **Payment page** (if you selected a course before registering)
   - **Login page** (if you registered without selecting a course)

**Post-Registration:**
- Check your email for the welcome message
- Log in with your email and password
- You're ready to browse and enroll in courses!

## 4. Browsing and Selecting Courses {#browsing-courses}

### Accessing the Course Catalog

**From Homepage:**
- View featured courses on the main page
- Click **"View All Courses"** or **"Browse Courses"**

**From Dashboard:**

### Course Information Display

Each course shows:
- **Course Title**: Name of the course (e.g., "Florida Basic Driver Improvement")
- **Description**: What the course covers
- **State Code**: Which state the course is approved for (FL, MO, TX, etc.)
- **Duration**: Total course length in hours
- **Price**: Course cost in USD
- **Course Type**: BDI (Basic Driver Improvement), ADI, etc.
- **Rating**: Average student rating (if available)
- **Reviews**: Number of student reviews

### Filtering and Searching

**Filter by State:**
1. Use the state dropdown filter
2. Select your state (e.g., Florida, Missouri)
3. Only courses approved for that state will display

**Search for Courses:**
1. Use the search bar at the top
2. Enter keywords (e.g., "defensive driving", "8-hour")
3. Press Enter or click the search icon

### Viewing Course Details

1. Click on any course card or **"View Details"** button
2. The course details page shows:
   - Full course description
   - Chapter breakdown
   - Requirements and prerequisites
   - Student reviews and ratings
   - Frequently asked questions
   - Enrollment button

**Course Details Page Sections:**
- **Overview**: What you'll learn
- **Requirements**: Prerequisites and eligibility
- **Chapters**: List of course modules
- **Reviews**: Student feedback and ratings
- **Pricing**: Cost breakdown and any discounts


### Understanding Course Types

**Florida Courses:**
- Stored in `florida_courses` table
- State-approved for Florida traffic citations
- Includes DICDS submission to Florida DHSMV
- Typically 4-hour or 8-hour formats

**Regular Courses:**
- Stored in `courses` table
- Available for multiple states
- State-specific compliance features
- Various duration options

### Making Your Selection

**Before Enrolling, Consider:**
- Is this course approved for your state?
- Does the duration meet your court requirements?
- Have you read the reviews?
- Do you understand the refund policy?

**Ready to Enroll:**
1. Click **"Enroll Now"** or **"Purchase Course"**
2. You'll be directed to the checkout page
3. Proceed to Section 5 (Payment and Enrollment)

## 5. Payment and Enrollment {#payment-enrollment}

**Step 1: Review Course Selection**
- Verify you've selected the correct course
- Check course title, state, and duration
- Review the price

**Step 2: Apply Coupon Code (Optional)**

2. Click **"Apply"**
3. The discount will be calculated and displayed
4. You'll see:

   - Discount Amount
   - Final Amount

- Codes may have expiration dates

- Some codes have usage limits

- Certified mail delivery

   - Or use cards linked to PayPal

Your confirmation email includes:

**Accessing "My Enrollments":**

## 6. Taking Your Course {#taking-course}

### Accessing the Course Player

**From My Enrollments:**
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to **"My Enrollments"**
3. Find your paid course
4. Click **"Access Course"** or **"Continue Learning"**

**From Email Link:**
1. Open your enrollment confirmation email
2. Click the **"Access Your Course"** link
3. You'll be logged in automatically (if not, log in first)

### Course Player Interface

**Main Components:**

**1. Navigation Sidebar (Left)**
- List of all chapters
- Chapter numbers and titles
- Completion checkmarks (✓) for finished chapters
- Current chapter highlighted
- Progress indicator
- Final Exam at the bottom

**2. Content Area (Center)**
- Chapter title
- Chapter content (text, images, videos)
- Media player (for video chapters)
- Scroll to read all content
- Action buttons at bottom

**3. Timer Display (Top Right)**
- Shows elapsed time for current chapter
- Required time remaining
- Progress bar
- Status indicator (In Progress / Complete)

**4. Progress Bar (Top)**
- Overall course completion percentage
- Visual progress indicator
- Updates as you complete chapters

### Understanding the Chapter Timer

**Why Timers Exist:**
- State compliance requirements
- Ensures adequate learning time
- Prevents rushing through material

**How Timers Work:**
1. Timer starts automatically when you open a chapter
2. Counts up from 00:00
3. Must reach the required time before proceeding
4. Timer continues even if you switch tabs (violation logged)
5. Green checkmark appears when time requirement is met

**Timer Requirements:**
- Each chapter has a minimum required time
- Typically 5-15 minutes per chapter
- Cannot skip or fast-forward
- Must keep the browser tab active
- Violations are logged (tab switches, page reloads)

**Timer Violations:**
The system detects and logs:
- Tab switches (switching to another browser tab)
- Window blur (clicking outside the browser)
- Page reloads (refreshing the page)
- Developer tools usage
- Multiple tabs open
- Time manipulation attempts

**Admin Users:**
- Administrators bypass timer restrictions
- Useful for course review and testing
- Violations are not logged for admin accounts


### Navigating Through Chapters

**Starting a Chapter:**
1. Click on a chapter in the sidebar
2. The chapter content loads in the center area
3. Timer starts automatically
4. Read/watch all content carefully

**Reading Chapter Content:**
- Scroll down to read all text
- View all images and diagrams
- Watch all videos completely
- Take notes if helpful
- Review key points

**Completing a Chapter:**
1. Read all content thoroughly
2. Wait for the timer to complete
3. The "Next Chapter" or "Take Quiz" button becomes enabled
4. Click the button to proceed
5. A checkmark (✓) appears next to the completed chapter

**Chapter Types:**

**1. Regular Chapters:**
- Text and multimedia content
- Timer requirement
- Quiz at the end (usually)
- Must complete to proceed

**2. Chapter Breaks:**
- Mandatory rest periods
- Appear between certain chapters
- Display break message
- Timer counts down
- Cannot skip

**3. Free Response Quizzes:**
- Appear between chapters
- Open-ended questions
- Type your answers
- Graded by instructor (if applicable)
- Must complete to proceed

**4. Final Exam:**
- Appears as the last "chapter"
- Comprehensive assessment
- Covered in Section 8

### Viewing Videos

**Video Player Controls:**
- **Play/Pause**: Start or stop the video
- **Volume**: Adjust sound level
- **Fullscreen**: Expand to full screen
- **Progress Bar**: See video progress
- **Playback Speed**: Some videos allow speed adjustment

**Video Requirements:**
- Must watch videos completely
- Cannot skip ahead
- Video completion tracked
- Some videos have minimum watch time

### Pausing and Resuming

**Taking a Break:**
1. Your progress is automatically saved
2. You can close the browser
3. Timer progress is saved
4. Chapter completion is saved
5. Quiz results are saved

**Resuming Later:**
1. Log back in to your account
2. Go to "My Enrollments"
3. Click "Continue Learning"
4. You'll return to where you left off
5. Timer resumes from where it stopped

**Important Notes:**
- Progress saves every 30 seconds
- Don't rely on browser back button
- Use the navigation sidebar
- Bookmark the course player page
- Keep your login credentials secure

### Progress Tracking

**What's Tracked:**
- Chapters completed
- Quiz scores
- Time spent per chapter
- Overall course progress
- Last activity date

**Viewing Your Progress:**
- Progress percentage at the top
- Checkmarks on completed chapters

## 7. Quizzes and Assessments {#quizzes}

### Chapter Quizzes

**When Quizzes Appear:**
- At the end of most chapters
- After completing the chapter timer
- Before proceeding to the next chapter
- Marked as "Take Quiz" button

**Quiz Format:**
- Multiple choice questions
- True/False questions
- Typically 5-10 questions per quiz
- One question displayed at a time
- Cannot go back to previous questions

### Taking a Quiz

**Step-by-Step Process:**

1. **Start the Quiz**
   - Click "Take Quiz" button
   - Quiz instructions appear
   - Number of questions shown
   - Time limit displayed (if applicable)
   - Click "Begin Quiz"

2. **Answer Questions**
   - Read each question carefully
   - Review all answer choices
   - Select your answer by clicking the radio button
   - Click "Next Question" to proceed
   - Cannot return to previous questions

3. **Submit Quiz**
   - After the last question, click "Submit Quiz"
   - Confirmation prompt appears
   - Click "Confirm" to submit
   - Quiz is graded immediately

4. **View Results**
   - Your score is displayed
   - Percentage correct shown
   - Pass/Fail status indicated
   - Correct answers revealed (usually)
   - Explanations provided (if available)


### Quiz Scoring

**Passing Requirements:**
- Typically 70-80% to pass (varies by course)
- Passing score displayed before starting
- Must pass to proceed to next chapter
- Scores contribute to overall quiz average

**Score Calculation:**
- Each question worth equal points
- Correct answers = points earned
- Percentage = (Correct / Total) × 100
- Example: 8 out of 10 correct = 80%

### Quiz Retakes

**If You Fail a Quiz:**
1. Review the correct answers and explanations
2. Study the chapter material again
3. Click "Retake Quiz" button
4. New questions may be presented (from question bank)
5. Unlimited retakes allowed (usually)
6. Best score is recorded

**Retake Strategy:**
- Review incorrect answers carefully
- Re-read relevant chapter sections
- Take notes on key concepts
- Don't rush - take your time
- Most students pass on second attempt

### Free Response Quizzes

**What Are They:**
- Open-ended questions requiring written answers
- Appear between certain chapters
- Test comprehension and critical thinking
- May require instructor grading

**How to Complete:**
1. Read each question carefully
2. Type your answer in the text box
3. Write clearly and completely
4. Check spelling and grammar
5. Click "Submit" when finished

**Grading:**
- May be auto-graded or instructor-graded
- Results may take 24-48 hours (if instructor-graded)
- You'll receive email notification when graded
- Must pass to proceed

### Quiz Tips for Success

**Before Starting:**
- Review chapter content thoroughly
- Take notes on key points
- Ensure stable internet connection
- Find a quiet place without distractions
- Have scratch paper handy

**During the Quiz:**
- Read each question completely
- Read all answer choices before selecting
- Eliminate obviously wrong answers
- Trust your first instinct (usually correct)
- Don't overthink simple questions
- Manage your time wisely

**After the Quiz:**
- Review correct answers and explanations
- Note any concepts you missed
- Study those areas before retaking
- Learn from mistakes

## 8. Final Exam {#final-exam}

### About the Final Exam

**Purpose:**
- Comprehensive assessment of course material
- Tests knowledge from all chapters
- Required to complete the course
- Determines certificate eligibility

**Format:**
- Multiple choice and True/False questions
- Typically 25-50 questions
- Questions drawn from entire course
- One question at a time
- Cannot go back to previous questions

**Requirements:**
- Must complete all chapters first
- Must pass all chapter quizzes
- Must meet minimum quiz average (if required)
- Must complete all chapter timers

### Accessing the Final Exam

**When Available:**
1. Complete all course chapters
2. Pass all chapter quizzes
3. Meet all timer requirements
4. "Final Exam" appears in chapter list
5. Click to begin

**Before Starting:**
- Review all chapter materials
- Review quiz questions and answers
- Take practice tests (if available)
- Ensure 60-90 minutes of uninterrupted time
- Stable internet connection required
- Find a quiet, distraction-free location

### Taking the Final Exam

**Step 1: Exam Instructions**
- Read all instructions carefully
- Note the number of questions
- Note the passing score (typically 80%)
- Note the time limit (if applicable)
- Understand retake policy
- Click "Begin Final Exam"

**Step 2: Answering Questions**
- Read each question thoroughly
- Review all answer choices
- Select your answer
- Click "Next Question"
- Cannot return to previous questions
- Progress indicator shows questions remaining

**Step 3: Review and Submit**
- After last question, review screen appears
- Shows number of questions answered
- Option to review flagged questions (if feature enabled)
- Click "Submit Final Exam"
- Confirmation prompt appears
- Click "Confirm Submission"

**Step 4: Immediate Results**
- Exam is graded instantly
- Your score is displayed
- Pass/Fail status shown
- Percentage correct shown
- Correct answers may be revealed


### Passing the Final Exam

**Passing Score:**
- Typically 80% or higher
- Varies by state and course
- Displayed before starting exam
- Must achieve to receive certificate

**If You Pass:**
1. Congratulations message appears
2. Course status changes to "Completed"
3. Certificate becomes available
4. Completion email sent
5. Proceed to certificate download (Section 9)

**If You Fail:**
1. Your score is displayed
2. Areas for improvement noted
3. Study recommendations provided
4. Retake option available
5. Review course materials before retaking

### Final Exam Retakes

**Retake Policy:**
- Number of attempts varies by course
- Typically 2-3 attempts allowed
- May require waiting period between attempts (24 hours)
- May require additional fee for extra attempts
- Contact support if you exhaust attempts

**Preparing for Retake:**
1. Review all course chapters
2. Focus on areas you missed
3. Retake chapter quizzes
4. Take notes on difficult concepts
5. Use study guides (if provided)
6. Wait the required time period
7. Ensure you're well-rested and focused

**Retake Process:**
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to course player
3. Click "Retake Final Exam"
4. New questions may be presented
5. Follow same process as first attempt

### Final Exam Tips

**Preparation:**
- Study all chapters thoroughly
- Review all quiz questions
- Take notes on key concepts
- Create flashcards for important terms
- Get adequate sleep before exam
- Eat a good meal before starting

**During the Exam:**
- Read questions carefully
- Don't rush - you have time
- Eliminate wrong answers first
- Trust your knowledge
- Don't second-guess yourself
- Stay calm and focused
- Take deep breaths if anxious

**Time Management:**
- Note the time limit
- Pace yourself evenly
- Don't spend too long on one question
- Keep track of time remaining
- Save difficult questions for last (if allowed)

### Certificate Information

**What's Included:**
- Your full legal name
- Course name and type
- Completion date
- Final exam score
- Certificate number (unique identifier)
- Driver's license number
- Date of birth
- Citation number (if applicable)
- Court information (if applicable)

### Downloading Your Certificate

**PDF Download:**
1. Click "Download PDF" button
2. Certificate generates as PDF
3. PDF downloads to your device
4. Save in a secure location
5. Print if needed

**Certificate Email:**
- PDF automatically emailed to your registered address
- Check spam/junk folder if not received
- Email includes certificate as attachment
- Keep email for your records

**Important Notes:**
- Download immediately after completion
- Save multiple copies
- Print a physical copy
- Keep digital and physical copies secure
- You may need to submit to court/insurance


### Certificate Verification

**Verification Hash:**
- Each certificate has unique verification code
- Courts/insurance can verify authenticity
- Verification URL provided on certificate
- Valid for official purposes

**How Verification Works:**
1. Certificate includes verification URL
2. Third parties visit the URL
3. Enter certificate number or hash
4. System confirms certificate is valid
5. Shows student name, course, completion date

### State Submission (Florida)

**Automatic DICDS Submission:**
- Florida certificates automatically submitted to DHSMV
- Submission via DICDS (Driver Improvement Course Data System)
- Typically processed within 24-48 hours
- Confirmation number provided
- Court receives notification electronically

**Submission Status:**
- Check "My Enrollments" for submission status
- Status options: Pending, Submitted, Confirmed, Failed
- Email notification when submitted
- Contact support if submission fails

### Account Access After Certificate Download

**Important Security Feature:**
- After downloading certificate, account access may be restricted
- This prevents unauthorized certificate access
- Protects your personal information
- Standard security practice

**What Happens:**
1. You download your certificate
2. System logs the download
3. Account may be locked after download
4. You'll be logged out automatically
5. Cannot log back in (security measure)

**Why This Happens:**
- Prevents certificate fraud
- Protects your identity
- Ensures one-time certificate issuance
- Complies with state regulations

**If You Need Access:**
- Contact support before downloading
- Request additional certificate copies first
- Save all needed information before download
- Support can provide assistance if needed

### Submitting Your Certificate

**To Court:**
1. Check your court's submission requirements
2. Some courts accept email submission
3. Some require physical mail
4. Some accept online portal upload
5. Submit before your due date
6. Keep proof of submission

**To Insurance Company:**
1. Contact your insurance provider
2. Ask about submission method
3. Email or mail certificate copy
4. Request confirmation of receipt
5. Ask about discount processing time
6. Follow up if needed

**Submission Tips:**
- Submit as soon as possible
- Don't wait until the last minute
- Keep copies of all correspondence
- Get confirmation of receipt
- Note submission date and method
- Follow up if no response in 1 week

## 13. Frequently Asked Questions {#faq}

### Registration & Account

**Q: Do I need to create an account to browse courses?**
A: No, you can browse courses without an account. However, you must create an account to enroll and take courses.

**Q: Can I use the same email for multiple accounts?**
A: No, each email address can only be associated with one account.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the reset instructions sent to your email.

**Q: Can I change my email address after registration?**
A: Yes, but you may need to verify the new email. Contact support for assistance.

**Q: Is my personal information secure?**
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data.

### Courses & Enrollment

**Q: How do I know which course to take?**
A: Check your citation or court paperwork for the required course type. You can also contact your court or our support team.

**Q: Can I take a course for a different state?**
A: You must take a course approved for the state where you received your citation.

**Q: How long do I have to complete the course?**
A: Check your court deadline. Most students have 30-90 days, but this varies by court.

**Q: Can I enroll in multiple courses?**
A: Yes, you can enroll in multiple courses, but each requires separate payment.

**Q: What if I miss my court deadline?**
A: Contact your court immediately. Late completion may result in additional penalties.

### Payment & Refunds

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) and PayPal.

**Q: Is payment secure?**
A: Yes, we use Stripe and PayPal for secure payment processing. We never store your card information.

**Q: Do you offer payment plans?**
A: Payment plans are not currently available. Full payment is required at enrollment.

**Q: What is your refund policy?**
A: Refunds are available within 7 days of purchase if you haven't started the course. Contact support for refund requests.

**Q: Can I get a refund after starting the course?**
A: Partial refunds may be available depending on progress. Contact support to discuss your situation.

### Taking the Course

**Q: How long does it take to complete the course?**
A: Course duration varies (4-8 hours typically). You can complete at your own pace within your deadline.

**Q: Can I pause and resume the course?**
A: Yes, your progress is automatically saved. You can log out and resume anytime.

**Q: Do I have to complete the course in one sitting?**
A: No, you can take breaks and complete the course over multiple sessions.

**Q: What if my internet connection drops?**
A: Your progress is saved every 30 seconds. Reconnect and continue where you left off.

**Q: Can I skip chapters?**
A: No, chapters must be completed in order. Each chapter has a minimum time requirement.

**Q: Why is there a timer on each chapter?**
A: State regulations require minimum time spent on each chapter to ensure adequate learning.

**Q: Can I use my phone or tablet?**
A: While possible, we recommend using a desktop or laptop for the best experience, especially for the final exam.

### Quizzes & Final Exam

**Q: How many questions are on each quiz?**
A: Chapter quizzes typically have 5-10 questions. The final exam has 25-50 questions.

**Q: What is the passing score?**
A: Chapter quizzes typically require 70%, and the final exam requires 80%. This may vary by course.

**Q: Can I retake quizzes?**
A: Yes, you can retake chapter quizzes unlimited times. Final exam retakes may be limited.

**Q: What happens if I fail the final exam?**
A: You can retake it after reviewing the material. Most courses allow 2-3 attempts.

**Q: Are quiz questions randomized?**
A: Yes, questions are drawn from a question bank, so each attempt may have different questions.

### Certificates

**Q: When will I receive my certificate?**
A: Your certificate is available immediately after passing the final exam.

**Q: How do I download my certificate?**
A: Go to "My Enrollments," find your completed course, and click "View Certificate," then "Download PDF."

**Q: Will my certificate be emailed to me?**
A: Yes, a PDF copy is automatically emailed to your registered email address.

**Q: Do I need to submit my certificate to the court?**
A: For Florida, submission is automatic. For other states, check with your court for submission requirements.

**Q: How long is my certificate valid?**
A: Certificate validity varies by state and purpose. Check your court paperwork or contact your court.

**Q: Can I get a replacement certificate?**
A: Contact support for replacement certificates. A fee may apply.

**Q: What if there's an error on my certificate?**
A: Contact support immediately. We'll issue a corrected certificate within 24-48 hours.

### Technical Issues

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions). Internet Explorer is not supported.

**Q: Why won't the video play?**
A: Check your internet connection, try a different browser, disable ad blockers, and ensure JavaScript is enabled.

**Q: The timer isn't working. What should I do?**
A: Don't switch tabs or refresh the page. If the timer freezes, contact support immediately.

**Q: My progress isn't saving. Help!**
A: Ensure stable internet connection and use the "Next Chapter" button. Contact support if the issue persists.
